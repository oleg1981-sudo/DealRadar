## 0. Executive summary

3. **Data loss (process, not code):** the pull destroyed the untracked, human-approved URL/slug spec package (v2, incl. the 49→25 red-team register), `tasks/`, and the 2026-06-28 audit files. Partially recovered under `/Users/danielmanzela/DealRadar/docs/recovered/2026-07-09/`; the v3 ground-source-of-truth survived but is untracked and **at risk until committed**. _**Post-audit update (same day):** the spec package + `tasks/plan.md`/`tasks/todo.md` were restored by the authoring session and **COMMITTED at `b53bb3e`** (2026-07-09 10:56), including the full register + `redteam-adjudication-full.json` — see "Post-audit update" at the end of this file._
